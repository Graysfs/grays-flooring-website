What Carpet Options
Are Available?

We offer everything from builder grade carpet for a clean, cost-effective finish all the way up to 65+ oz premium carpet for those who want the ultimate in softness, durability, and luxury underfoot.

The face weight of a carpet, measured in ounces per square yard, is one of the most important indicators of quality. The higher the face weight, the denser and more resilient the carpet. A denser carpet holds up better to heavy foot traffic, resists matting and crushing, and maintains its appearance far longer. If you are carpeting a bedroom that sees light use, a mid-range option performs beautifully. If you are carpeting a high-traffic living room or a home with kids and pets, investing in a higher face weight pays for itself in longevity.

Why Does the
Pad Matter So Much?

Even the best carpet is only as good as the pad beneath it. The pad is what gives carpet that plush, cushioned feel underfoot, but more importantly, it acts as a support layer that protects the carpet backing, absorbs impact, and significantly extends the life of your floor.

We recommend a minimum of half-inch, 8 lb density padding on every installation. A quality pad reduces wear on the carpet fibers from below, insulates against temperature and sound, and makes the entire floor feel more substantial. Skimping on pad to save a few dollars upfront is one of the most common ways a carpet installation fails early. It is a compromise we refuse to make.

We also handle all tack strip installation, seaming, transitions between carpet and other flooring types, and stairs. Every edge is stretched tight and every seam is set to be invisible.
