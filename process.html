How It Works

Simple Process,
Beautiful Results

From the first phone call to the final walkthrough, we handle every step with care and precision. Here is exactly what to expect when you work with Gray's Flooring.

1Step One

Hands-On
Assessment & Quote

Before we quote anything, we show up. We get down on the floor, pull back edges, check what is underneath, and assess the real condition of your subfloor and existing material.

No estimates over the phone based on guesses. You get a clear, itemized quote based on what we actually see.

Free · No Obligation · Usually Within 24 Hours
2Step Two

Demo & Subfloor
Inspection

Before any new flooring goes down, the old material comes up. We carefully remove existing flooring, carpet, or tile and inspect the subfloor underneath for damage, soft spots, or moisture issues.

Any problems found at this stage are addressed before installation begins. This foundational work is what separates a floor that lasts decades from one that fails in five years.

Carpet Removal · Tile Demo · Subfloor Inspection
3Step Three

Subfloor Leveling
& Prep

Not every job requires leveling, but when it does, we are fully equipped to handle it. We apply self-leveling compound to meet Arizona state and manufacturer installation standards, ensuring your new floor has a solid, flat foundation.

You can see the mirror-like result in this photo. No dips, no high spots, no hollow clicks underfoot. When leveling is needed, we do not skip it.

Self-Leveling Compound · Moisture Barrier · Flat Surface Prep
4Step Four

Precision
Installation

This is where the craftsmanship shows. Each tile is set in quality mortar, hand laid by expert installers.

Red leveling clips and wedges are used to keep every tile perfectly flush with its neighbor, eliminating lippage. The result is a floor that is flat, strong, and built to last decades.

Ditra Membrane · Leveling System · Professional Grade
5Step Five

The Finished
Floor Reveal

Once installation is complete, we clean up completely and do a full quality check before calling you in. The transformation you see here, large format tile running through an entire open-plan kitchen and living space, is typical of a full installation.

Trim, transitions, and baseboards are fitted last to give the floor a clean, professional edge in every room.

LVP · Hardwood · Tile · Full Room Installation
6Step Six

Final Walkthrough
& Custom Detail

The last step is a thorough walkthrough together. We inspect every transition, every grout line, every edge. You should not find anything we have not already caught.

For specialty projects like this custom tile shower with large format tile walls, a pebble stone floor, and a built-in niche, we walk through every detail until you are completely satisfied. We do not consider a job done until you do.

Final Inspection · Custom Showers · 100% Satisfaction
