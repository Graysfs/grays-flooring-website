What Is It
Why Choose
Tile Flooring?

Tile is one of the most proven flooring materials in the world, and for good reason. It is completely waterproof, scratch resistant, and virtually indestructible under normal use. In Arizona's climate it also stays naturally cool underfoot, making it a popular choice for living rooms, kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, and outdoor transitions.

Modern tile comes in an enormous range of styles, sizes, and finishes. Large format tiles create a seamless, expansive look that makes rooms feel bigger. Wood-look tiles give you the warmth of hardwood with the durability of ceramic or porcelain. Marble-look tiles deliver a high-end aesthetic at a fraction of the cost of natural stone. Whatever the vision, there is a tile that fits it.

How It Works
What Goes Into
a Proper Installation?

The quality of a tile floor depends almost entirely on what happens before the tile goes down. We use premium mortar for superior bonding strength, ensuring each tile is firmly set and resistant to cracking, shifting, or lifting over time. The mortar beneath your tile is just as important as the tile itself. It is the foundation that determines whether your floor looks perfect ten years from now or starts showing problems in two.

Throughout every installation we use professional leveling clips and wedges. This system holds each tile at a precise, uniform height while the mortar cures, eliminating lippage, the uneven edges between tiles that are the most common sign of poor workmanship. The result is a surface that looks razor sharp, feels perfectly smooth underfoot, and reflects the care that goes into every job we do.

We handle all grout work, caulking at transitions and corners, and clean up completely before the final walkthrough. What you get is a finished floor, not just set tiles.
