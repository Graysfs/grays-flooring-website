What Is It
What Makes a Shower
Truly Custom?

A custom tile shower is built from scratch, in place, to the exact dimensions and design of your choosing. Unlike prefabricated inserts that come in fixed sizes and limited styles, a custom shower lets you choose every element, from the tile pattern and grout color to the placement of niches, benches, and drains.

Whether you are envisioning a spa-like retreat with floor-to-ceiling large format tile, a sleek walk-in with a linear drain, a bold statement wall in a contrasting material, or a classic subway tile design with handcrafted niches, we have the skills and experience to bring it to life. Every shower we build is one of a kind.

How It Works
How Is a Custom
Shower Built?

Waterproofing is the foundation of every shower we build. A beautiful tile job means nothing without a properly waterproofed substrate behind it. Before a single tile is ever set, we treat every surface, walls, floor, and transitions, protecting your home from moisture damage for decades to come. This step is non-negotiable for us regardless of the scope of the project.

Once the substrate is prepared, we use premium mortar and professional leveling clips throughout the entire installation, ensuring every tile is securely bonded and perfectly flush. From large format wall tiles to intricate mosaic shower floors, our process guarantees tight, consistent grout joints and a surface completely free of lippage or uneven edges.

We also build all custom features in place, including built-in niches, shower benches, curbs, and decorative accents. Everything is coordinated so the finished shower looks intentional and complete, not assembled from separate parts.
