What We Fix
What Types of Repairs
Do You Handle?

We repair all three major flooring types. For LVP and vinyl plank, we replace individual damaged planks, whether caused by heavy impact, deep scratches, or lifting edges, seamlessly blending the repair into your existing floor so it looks as good as new.

For tile, we replace cracked, chipped, or broken tiles with precision, ensuring each replacement is properly set with premium mortar and leveled flush with the surrounding surface. A single bad tile does not have to mean a whole new floor.

For carpet, we offer professional re-stretching to eliminate ripples, buckles, and loose areas that develop over time from normal wear and foot traffic. Re-stretching restores the appearance of your carpet and extends its life by preventing further wear and fraying along the affected areas. We also patch damaged sections of carpet, including pet damage, cuts, and burns, matching the existing pile as closely as possible for a result you can barely find.

Real Example
Pet Damage Is
More Common Than You Think

This is one of our favorite types of calls. A customer reached out after their cat had worked over a section of carpet near a doorway, pulling it up, fraying the edges, and leaving an obvious patch of damage that looked far worse than it was.

We came out, assessed the damage, cut out the affected section, and patched it with matching material, re-stretching the surrounding carpet and tucking the edges back cleanly under the door transition. The repair blended in almost perfectly and saved the customer the cost of replacing the entire room.

If your floor has a problem area, there is a good chance it can be fixed without starting over. Give us a call or fill out the form below and we will take a look.
