What Is It
What is Luxury
Vinyl Plank?

Luxury Vinyl Plank is a multi-layer synthetic flooring product engineered to look and feel like real hardwood. Each plank features a high-resolution photographic layer printed with realistic wood grain patterns, topped with a durable wear layer that protects the surface from scratches, dents, and daily wear.

Unlike real hardwood, LVP is completely waterproof. It will not warp, swell, buckle, or cup when exposed to humidity fluctuations or direct water contact, making it an ideal choice for kitchens, bathrooms, laundry rooms, and any space where moisture is a daily reality. Whether it is a spill, steam, or Arizona's seasonal humidity swings, LVP holds its shape and appearance year after year.

How It Works
What Does
Installation Involve?

LVP is installed as a floating floor, meaning the planks click and lock together without being glued or nailed to the subfloor. This makes it faster to install and easier to replace individual planks if needed down the road.

When installed over concrete subfloors, we include a 6-mil vapor barrier beneath the flooring. Concrete is naturally porous and can release moisture vapor over time. The vapor barrier acts as a protective layer, blocking that moisture from migrating up into your new floor and ensuring a long-lasting, stable installation. On wood subfloors we assess and address any soft spots, squeaks, or unevenness before any planks go down.

We also handle all trim work, transitions between rooms, and baseboards so the finished result looks completely clean and professional throughout your home.
